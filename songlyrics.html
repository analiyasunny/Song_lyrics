FROZEN

About Song

"Let It Go" is a song from Disney's "Frozen," sung by Elsa, voiced by Idina Menzel. It's about Elsa embracing her powers and letting go of fear, becoming her true self. The song won an Academy Award and became a cultural phenomenon.

About Author

Kristen Anderson-Lopez and Robert Lopez are the talented \husband-and-wife duo behind "Let It Go." Robert co-created acclaimed musicals like "Avenue Q" and "The Book of Mormon," earning him an EGOT. Kristen is a lyricist known for her work in musicals. Together, they won an Oscar for the song, cementing their place in entertainment history.
